Sample Use Case: Fingertips for eCommerce Marketplace Sellers

Background

  • An e-commerce company struggled with tracking performance and managing inventory effectively.
  • lack of real-time visibility into critical metrics hindered decision-making.
  • Manual reporting made it difficult to optimize operations.

Challenges

  • Data was scattered across sales, inventory, pricing, and liabilities systems.
  • Manual analysis limited actionable insights and slowed responsiveness.
  • Decisions were often delayed due to lack of real-time visibility.
  • The business faced inefficiencies that reduced competitiveness.

Solution: Fingertips with Power BI

  • An analytics platform provided a centralized KPI dashboard for visibility.
  • Dynamic visualizations enabled trend detection and performance monitoring.

Benefit

  • Delivered a unified view of critical business metrics and trends.
  • Enabled real-time, data-driven strategic and operational decisions.
  • Automated reporting cut manual workload and improved efficiency.
  • Optimized inventory, pricing, and liabilities management to drive growth.

Use Case: Logistics Payment Reconciliation

Background

  • Ecommerce marketplace Operates with 10–15 logistics partners, managing thousands of shipments daily.
  • Variables include pin code, shipment mode, weight, zone, and service level.

Challenges

  • Contract complexity – thousands of rate combinations, frequently changing
  • Diverse scenarios with calculations for partshipments, failed deliveries, returns, surcharges and COD
  • Huge volume needing validation with lengthy resolution cycles

Solution: AI Based Logistics Reconciliation Tool

  • Automated data ingestion, dynamic contract management, AI Powered audit engine that automates dispute handling and tracks resolution

Benefit

Error Detection & Cost Recovery

Identified significant overpayments, safeguarding 2–5% of monthly freight spend. Enhanced transparency and reduced friction.

Efficiency Boost

Reduced reconciliation time from weeks to 1–2 days, automating 75–85% of invoice lines.
