PO Flow using AI/ML

Process Flow: Various departments raise PO requests via email → AI engine reads and initiates approvals → Approvers approve via email link or app → Post approval creates PO in SAP with full tracking

Minimal disruption - Reduction of work on for end users

Highly compliant - PO process compliant with line details

End to End trackability - Full visibility of PO requests and timelines

Invoice Processing flow – using AI/ML

Process Flow: Supplier sends invoices → Invoice received digitally → AI extracts line level details → Automated 3-way match & exception management → SAP invoice created

Fully digitized extraction & system enabled

Automated 3-way match of invoices

Reduced manual efforts up to 60% on process

Seamless integration into SAP with line level data

End to End trackability of GRN, Invoices & improved timeliness as it can be fully automated

Sample Use Case: Fingertips for eCommerce Marketplace Sellers

Background

An e-commerce company struggled with tracking performance and managing inventory effectively. Manual reporting made it difficult to optimize operations, impacting strategic planning and growth.

Challenges

Data scattered across systems. Manual analysis limited actionable insights. Delayed decisions due to lack of real-time visibility. Business faced inefficiencies reducing competitiveness.

Solution: Fingertips with Power BI

Analytics platform provided centralized KPI dashboard. Dynamic visualizations enabled trend detection and performance monitoring.

Benefit

Unified view of critical metrics. Real-time, data-driven strategic and operational decisions. Automated reporting reduced workload. Optimized inventory and pricing management to drive growth.

Use Case: Logistics Payment Reconciliation

Background

Ecommerce marketplace operates with 10-15 logistics partners, managing thousands of shipments daily with variables including pin code, shipment mode, weight, zone, and service level.

Challenges

Contract complexity with thousands of rate combinations. Diverse scenarios with calculations for partshipments, failed deliveries, returns, surcharges and COD. Huge volume needing validation.

Solution: AI Based Logistics Reconciliation Tool

Automated data ingestion, dynamic contract management, AI Powered audit engine automating dispute handling and tracking resolution.

Benefit

Error Detection & Cost Recovery: Identified overpayments safeguarding 2-5% of monthly freight. Efficiency Boost: Reduced time from weeks to 1-2 days, automating 75-85% of invoices.
